Pizza

Homepage

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta.



Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Lukewarm water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Muzzarella cheez

Steps:

  1. Start with dough, mix amounts of water, flour, yeast and a pinch of salt
  2. Set aside to rest until double in size
  3. Cut into small balls and begin forming pizza, by rolling out into circular shape
  4. Prepare the sauce and toppingS
  5. Add topings and a drizzle of olive oil
  6. Bake the pizza
  7. Cool the pizza
  8. Enjoy :) --
